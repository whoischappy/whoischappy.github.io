Chappy’s origins and motives are still unknown. There is also no documentation of it ever speaking or attempting to communicate with anyone besides a potential host.
Chappy conducts possessions utilizing a two-step method. Once a target is selected, Chappy takes the form of said target and is only visible to the imminent host. They are then psychologically tormented to the point of near madness and extreme vulnerability
One this point is reached, Chappy will lay out his signature suit. The host is subsequently influenced by Chappy to put it on. Once the suit is donned, Chappy gains total control of the host's mind and body. He feeds off of their soul until death, after which Chappy seeks out his next target.
IMPORTANT INFORMATION:
  • Only one person has escaped Chappy's hunt.
  • Chappy cannot be physically harmed, however it is believed that if you destroy HIS mirror, you are freed.
  • Chappy cannot be seen by anyone but a potential host.
  • Chappy CAN be captured on camera.
  • Chappy CAN interact with technology and real-world objects.
  • Chappy seems to enjoy toying with potential hosts.
  • If you ever come across Chappy’s Cabin, turn back immediately. We strongly suggest that NO ONE goes there for any reason whatsoever.
